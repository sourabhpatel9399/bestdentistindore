The Experts

The People Behind Your Smile

A team of highly qualified dental professionals committed to excellence

Dr. Pooja

Founder & Lead Dentist
10+ Years of Experience

Dr. Pooja is the founder and lead dentist at Pearly White Dental Clinic. With over 10 years of clinical excellence, she is passionate about delivering compassionate, precision-driven dental care. She specializes in Endodontics, cosmetic dentistry, and complete smile makeovers. Her patient-first philosophy and dedication to using the latest dental technology have made Pearly White Indore's most trusted dental clinic. Under her leadership, the clinic has served over 5000 happy patients with outstanding results.

Dr. Atul Kala

Oral & Maxillofacial Surgeon
10+ Years of Experience

Dr. Atul Kala is an experienced Oral & Maxillofacial Surgeon with over 10 years of clinical expertise in advanced dental and facial surgical procedures. He specializes in treatments such as dental implants, wisdom tooth removal, facial trauma management, and other complex oral surgeries. Known for his precision and patient-centered approach, Dr. Kala focuses on providing safe, comfortable, and modern dental care using advanced techniques and equipment. His goal is to ensure every patient receives the highest standard of treatment with minimal discomfort and long-lasting results.

Dr. Bhuwan Saklecha

Orthodontist
20+ Years of Experience

Dr. Bhuwan Saklecha is a highly accomplished Orthodontist with over 20 years of expertise in creating perfectly aligned smiles. He holds a B.D.S and M.D.S in Orthodontics and is a Certified Invisalign Provider — bringing world-class aligner therapy to Indore. He currently serves as Professor and Head of Department of Orthodontics at Guru Govind Singh Dental College, Burhanpur. His vast academic and clinical experience ensures that every patient receives the most advanced and evidence-based orthodontic treatment available.

Dr. Suyash Jain

Pedodontist & Child Specialist
13+ Years of Experience

Dr. Suyash Jain is a dedicated Pedodontist and Child Dental Specialist with over 13 years of experience in caring for young smiles. He holds a B.D.S and M.D.S in Pedodontics and is known for his exceptionally gentle and child-friendly approach that puts even the most anxious kids at ease. Dr. Jain focuses on preventive dental care, early orthodontic intervention, and building positive dental habits in children from an early age — ensuring a lifetime of healthy, confident smiles.

Prof.Dr.Hemant Gadge.

Pedodontist & Child Specialist
13+ Years of Experience

Dr. Hemant Gadge is a dedicated MDS Prosthodontist and Implantologist with 13 years of experience in providing quality dental care. He holds a B.D.S and M.D.S degree and is known for his gentle and patient-friendly approach that helps patients feel comfortable during treatment. Dr. Gadge focuses on delivering effective dental treatments while ensuring a comfortable and positive experience for every patient, helping them maintain healthy and confident smiles.
